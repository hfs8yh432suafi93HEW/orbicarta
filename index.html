orbicarta
n. /ˌɔː.bɪ.ˈkɑː.tə/
A topographic atlas of an orbital system.
etymology From Latin orbis (circle, orbit, bounded world) + carta (map, chart).
in use "The orbicarta of Jupiter showed the terrain under Titan's thick atmosphere."
coinage record Coined by William Nicholas Carter Guy on 25 April, 2026.
